About BANY

We at BANY DEVELOPMENT AND CONSTRUCTION are committed to excellence, innovation and creativity

BANY don’t only build residential projects, we build integrated living communities based on a streamlined ecosystem of facility management that’s for the first time to be presented among stand-alone projects (separate plots) and mini compounds in Egypt.

BANY is maintaining the highest standards of construction quality to create an integrated residential community for its customers. We believe that the needs of individuals are different and sophisticated. Our Architectures and Design Teams strive to ensure the best use of spaces through smart architectural designs and apply the highest standards of quality utilizing best materials available in the market.
